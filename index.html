About John

John is an enneagram type 5 - an observer who seeks understanding of himself and the world through analysis and imagination. He delves into his rich inner world of concepts and abstractions, and expresses his wondrous ideas through pixel art and programming.

As a child, John spent hours studying pixel art in classic video games. He was mesmerized by the nostalgic graphics and chiptune sounds that could create immersive virtual worlds and game mechanics.

Today, John brings this retro aesthetic into his own creations. He combines his love of pixel art, music, and interactive code to build one-of-a-kind browser-based games and animations.

When he's not creating pixel worlds, John is learning about psychology, philosophy, and game design. He is fascinated by what makes things - and people - tick. He aims to better understand the human experience through his introspective lens.

John hopes his pixel art and games provide others with moments of wonder, imagination, and nostalgia for retro computing.
